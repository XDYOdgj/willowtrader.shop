Starborn CMNTFAST Fasteners for Cement Boards 100 PCS

USD16.65
Starborn CMNTFAST Fasteners for Cement Boards are epoxy coated, 2-1/4" length, 100 PCS. Designed to reduce cracking and mushrooming, they offer a secure grip with a patented drive system. Ideal for Hardiebacker and equivalent cement boards, ensuring smooth and flush installation.

  • Reviews

  Starborn CMNTFAST Fasteners are designed to provide reliable and durable fastening solutions for cement boards. Their epoxy coating ensures a strong bond, offering enhanced resistance to moisture and corrosion. The cross-cut knurl on the fastener helps grind the cement board surface, minimizing cracking and mushrooming defects during installation. The serrated ribs beneath the head ensure a smooth, flush finish, making them ideal for professional applications. With a patented drive system, these fasteners eliminate common issues like cam-outs and wobble, delivering a secure and stable installation every time.
  Whether you're working with Hardiebacker, Durock, Wonderboard, or any equivalent cement board, Starborn CMNTFAST Fasteners are the perfect choice. Their versatility makes them suitable for a wide range of construction projects, from residential to commercial. The high-low thread design allows for a secure grip on the material, ensuring long-lasting stability. The 100-piece quantity is perfect for bulk use, offering efficiency and cost-effectiveness. These fasteners are a must-have for anyone looking for quality and reliability in their cement board installations.
